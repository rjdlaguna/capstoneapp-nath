Delayed Registration of Death

LIST OF REQUIREMENTS
  • Mandatory Documents
  • Duly accomplished and signed Death Certificate Form
  • Resident Certificate / Cedula
  • Less than 1 year from death
  • Official Receipt of Certification from Funeral Parlor
  • More than 1 year from death
  • PSA Certificate of No Registration
  • Official Receipt of Certification from Funeral Parlor
  • Process Overview
  • 1. Review of requirements & interview
  • 2. Payment at City Treasurer's Office
  • 3. Mandatory 10-day posting period
Registration Fee: ₱250.00
Processing Time: 10 Days Posting
