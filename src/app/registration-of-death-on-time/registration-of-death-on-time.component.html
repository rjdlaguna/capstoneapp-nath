On-Time Registration of Death

LIST OF REQUIREMENTS
  • Mandatory Document
  • Duly accomplished and signed Death Certificate (MF 103)
  • Authorized Reporters
  • Physician who last attended the deceased
  • Nearest relative or person with knowledge of the death
  • Funeral Provider
  • Permit Fees
  • Burial Permit: ₱50.00
  • Cremation Permit: ₱500.00
  • Transfer Permit: ₱100.00
  • Disinterment Permit: ₱75.00
  • Process Highlights
  • 1. Submit document signed by Physician & Embalmer (5 mins)
  • 2. Payment at Windows 13-19 (5 mins)
  • 3. Signature and Registry Number Assignment (5 mins)
Application Fee: ₱50.00 - ₱500.00
Processing Time: 20 Minutes
