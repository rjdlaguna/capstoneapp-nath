Delayed Registration of Marriage

LIST OF REQUIREMENTS
  • Mandatory Document
  • Duly accomplished Marriage Certificate (MF 102)
  • Less than 1 year from marriage
  • Unregistered Marriage Contract (MF 102) in 4 copies
  • PSA Certificate of No Marriage (CENOMAR)
  • Marriage License or Affidavit of Cohabitation
  • More than 1 year from marriage
  • PSA Certificate of No Marriage (CENOMAR)
  • PSA Certificate of No Registration
  • Resident Certificate/Cedula of Informant
  • Service Schedule
  • Monday - Friday | 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM (No noon break)
